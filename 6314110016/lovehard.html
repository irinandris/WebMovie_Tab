LOVEHARD (2021)









STORY

Love Hard is the story of a beautiful Natalie who has a hobby of swiping on dating apps and she has never succeeded in getting good love through dating. But that led to her getting a job as a columnist for Disastrous Dates at a publishing house. and when she starts swiping the app again to find content She caught the eye of Josh, a 30-year-old half-Chinese-American man. From the small town of Lake Placid in New York, she talked to him right up to his throat. She fell in love with George. And she decided to fly to surprise him home.

DIRECTOR

Hernán Jiménez
